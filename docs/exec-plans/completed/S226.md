# Exec Plan: S226 -- Multi-hint golden schema

**Phase:** V3 Phase 8 - Evaluation and Correctness
**Status:** complete

## Summary

S226 lets retrieval goldens use multiple valid passage hints for one question.
This fixes false misses where a book repeats the same fact in parallel passages:
the retriever can surface a correct alternate passage even when the original
single hint is absent from top-5.

## Implementation

- `evals/run_eval.py`
  - `GoldenEntry.context_hint` accepts `str | list[str]`.
  - Bare strings are coerced to single-element lists.
  - Empty lists and non-string list elements raise `ValidationError`.
  - `compute_hit_rate_5` scores a hit when any alternate appears in any top-5 chunk.
  - `compute_mrr` uses the rank of the first chunk matching any alternate.
- `evals/audit_golden.py`
  - Coerces hints to list form.
  - Reports per-alternate `pass` / `miss` state.
- `backend/tests/test_eval_metrics.py`
  - Covers schema coercion/rejection, multi-hint HR@5, multi-hint MRR, and string backwards compatibility.
- `scripts/smoke/S226.sh`
  - Pure-Python smoke for schema, metrics, curated datasets, and string-form backwards compatibility.

## Curated Alternate Hints

### `book_time_machine.jsonl`

1. Four dimensions of real bodies
   - Alternate: `There are really four dimensions, three which we call the three planes of Space, and a fourth, Time`
   - Rationale: Same four-dimension claim stated before the later "real body" formulation.
2. White Sphinx pedestal
   - Alternate: `taken it into the hollow pedestal of the White Sphinx`
   - Rationale: Same fact as "Time Machine was inside that pedestal", phrased as the Time Traveller's inference.
3. Consciousness and Time
   - Alternate: `consciousness moves intermittently in one direction along the latter`
   - Rationale: Same argument about consciousness moving along Time, restated during the time-travel debate.
4. Dimensions of space
   - Alternate: `any real body must have extension in _four_ directions: it must have Length, Breadth, Thickness`
   - Rationale: Same answer as the "three planes of Space, and a fourth, Time" passage.
5. Time versus Space
   - Alternate: `consciousness moves intermittently in one direction along the latter`
   - Rationale: Same Time/Space distinction appears in both narrator summary and dialogue.

### `book_odyssey.jsonl`

1. Penelope's web
   - Alternate: `used to keep working at my great web all day long, but at night I would unpick the stitches again by torch light`
   - Rationale: Book XIX first-person version of the same trick described in Book II.
2. Noman/Nobody
   - Alternate: `Noman is killing me by fraud; no man is killing me by force`
   - Rationale: Same false-name fact confirmed by the Cyclops repeating it.
3. Blinding Polyphemus
   - Alternate: `even thus did the Cyclops’ eye hiss round the beam of olive wood`
   - Rationale: Same blinding action continued in the simile immediately after the main action.
4. Sirens protection
   - Alternate: `pass these Sirens by, and stop your men’s ears with wax that none of them may hear`
   - Rationale: Circe's instruction appears both in preview and in direct command form.
5. Bound at the mast
   - Alternate: `they must lash the rope’s ends to the mast itself, that you may have the pleasure of listening`
   - Rationale: Same mast-restraint fact appears in Circe's instructions and the later execution.
