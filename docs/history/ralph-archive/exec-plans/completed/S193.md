# Exec Plan: S193 -- Glossary: persistent storage, categorization, and regeneration

**Phase:** V3 Phase 5 - Reading Experience and Chat Intelligence
**Priority:** 33
**Status:** in-progress

## Goal

The existing glossary feature calls the LLM on every click with no persistence, no error feedback, and no category information. This story adds `GlossaryTermModel` to SQLite, updates `ExplainService.extract_glossary()` to upsert terms with category and section linkage, adds three new endpoints (cached GET, persistent POST, DELETE term), and rewrites `GlossaryPanel` in `DocumentReader.tsx` to load from cache on mount, show loading/error/empty states, support category badges, search across term and definition, allow regeneration with merge semantics, and scroll the left panel to a term's first section mention.
